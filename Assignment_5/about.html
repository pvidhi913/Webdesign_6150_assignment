About US

Anytime Fitness is India's first chain of Super-speciality Physiotherapy, Fitness & Rehabilitation Centers. Since our establishment in 2007, we have been revolutionizing the field of Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation through expert skills, ultimate technologies, and a compassionate approach. Our mission is to bring the best practices in the rehab field by raising awareness about the vital role of physiotherapy as the first line of management for various disease conditions.
